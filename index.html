Our Founder

Alh. Momodou Turo Darboe

Alh. Momodou Turo Darboe is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of the Vision Development Foundation (VDF). He is a respected Gambian businessman and philanthropist with a long-standing commitment to community development, social justice, and poverty reduction.

Under his leadership, VDF was established in 2006 as a charitable non-governmental organization registered in The Gambia and affiliated with TANGO, The Association of Non-Governmental Organizations. His vision continues to guide the foundation’s work in empowering women, youths, and vulnerable communities through sustainable development initiatives.

Who We Are

Committed to dignity and sustainable development

Mission

VDF responds to the needs of the less privileged, particularly women, youths and children in The Gambia by creating opportunities for skill acquisition, addressing environmental degradation, providing access to micro-credit and enterprise development. We support education, health, social care, farmers, entrepreneurship, safe drinking water, urban regeneration, humanitarian and relief services, while promoting peace building, conflict resolution and national development.

Vision

A peaceful society in which opportunities exist for self-actualization geared towards poverty reduction, particularly for the impoverished and indigent. A Gambia without poverty and injustice where every person enjoys the right to a life of dignity.

Our Objectives

  • Support selected women groups to run revolving funds
  • Support micro finance and credit schemes for vulnerable groups
  • Support development and maintenance of public infrastructure
  • Provide ease of access to education, particularly the girl child
  • Provide management and leadership training for women and youth groups
  • Support life and livelihood skills development programmes among women

Our Aims and Goals

  • Increase income earning capacities and opportunities for the poor
  • Improve health and welfare among vulnerable communities
  • Enhance national security and public facilities
  • Foster self-reliance among VDF project beneficiaries
  • Collaborate with local authorities and NGOs in development

Sectors

Our Key Priority Sectors

Areas where we create lasting impact

Education

Vision Development Foundation supports access to quality education, with special focus on vulnerable children and the girl child. Through scholarships, school support programmes, skills training and capacity building, we aim to reduce illiteracy and empower communities with knowledge for sustainable development.

Healthcare

We work to improve access to basic healthcare services, especially for women, children and underserved communities. Our initiatives include health awareness campaigns, community outreach, and support for medical facilities to improve overall wellbeing.

Water and Sanitation

Access to safe drinking water is essential to human dignity. VDF supports water projects such as boreholes and sanitation initiatives that reduce waterborne diseases and improve living conditions in rural and urban communities.

Environment

Vision Development Foundation is committed to environmental protection and climate resilience. Our environmental programmes focus on addressing environmental degradation through community sensitization, tree planting, waste management initiatives and sustainable environmental practices. These efforts aim to protect natural resources and promote a healthier environment for present and future generations.

Agriculture and Livelihoods

Agriculture remains a key source of livelihood for many communities in The Gambia. VDF supports farmers and vulnerable households through agricultural training, access to inputs, and livelihood development programmes. Our goal is to strengthen food security, increase income generation and improve resilience among rural and peri-urban communities.

Youth Empowerment

VDF recognizes youth as drivers of sustainable development. Our youth empowerment programmes focus on skills development, leadership training, entrepreneurship support and civic engagement. By equipping young people with practical skills and opportunities, we promote self-reliance, innovation and positive social change.
